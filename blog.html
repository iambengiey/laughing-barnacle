Why B2B VAS needs country isolation

Country isolation is not just a compliance checkbox. It enables per-country operational ownership, regulatory reporting, and safer change windows for each corridor.

  • Dedicated runtime tenancy per jurisdiction
  • Country-specific runbooks and SLOs
  • Clearer incident response boundaries

Zero-trust integration patterns for regulated corridors

Zero-trust integration keeps sensitive payloads protected while maintaining observability and auditability.

  • Field-level encryption and tokenisation
  • Audit logs without storing payloads
  • Strict least-privilege access controls
