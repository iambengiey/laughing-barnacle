Primary product

OnCloud VAS Switching Platform (Africa B2B)

One integration point for enterprises to reach multiple VAS providers per country.

Features

  • API routing, orchestration, and compliance tooling
  • Country-isolated runtime tenancy and governance
  • Real-time monitoring and auditable operational logs
  • Partner custody model: OnCloud never holds customer funds

Architecture overview

  • Enterprise clients connect to the OnCloud Switch
  • Country runtimes enforce data residency and isolation
  • Provider connectors route to regulated partners
  • Policy engine, KMS/HSM, and telemetry ensure compliance

Compliance stance

  • Encryption in transit and at rest by default
  • Data minimisation with field-level encryption and tokenisation
  • Auditable logs without storing sensitive payloads
  • Least-privilege and zero-trust access controls

Country isolation model

  • Dedicated orgs, repos, and runtime boundaries per country
  • Country-specific operational ownership and access policies
  • Blue/green releases and rollback per jurisdiction
  • Per-country observability and SLA reporting
